Our Story

A Legacy Forged
in Flavour

The Royal Taste was born in 1998 from a single conviction: that a great meal is the highest form of hospitality. Our founders, inspired by classical French technique and the bold spice routes of the East, set out to create a table where the two worlds meet without compromise.

Over two decades and two Michelin stars later, that conviction remains unchanged. Every plate that leaves our kitchen carries the weight of tradition and the spark of invention.

Marcus Laurent Executive Chef & Co-Founder

"I cook to make people feel something they cannot name."
